Saucy Onion Gravy Beef Meatballs & Crushed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • British beef mince (125g) x0
  • Tenderstem broccoli (160g)
  • Gravy base (14g)
  • Tenderstem broccoli (80g) x0
  • Cornflour (1tsp)
  • Onion marmalade (20g)
  • Tomato paste (16g)
  • Garlic clove
  • British beef mince (250g)
  • Tomato paste (32g) x0
  • Brown onion
  • White potato x3


Instructions



Step 1

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick with a matching lid) over a high heat with a drizzle of vegetable oil Tear off pieces of your beef mince, roughly shape into small meatballs (about 6 per person!) and add directly to the pan with a pinch of salt and cook for 2 min on each side or until browned Tip: Cooking for 4 or more? Use 2 pans!





Step 2

Boil a full kettle Peel and slice your brown onion[s] Chop your potatoes (skins on) into large, bite-sized pieces Chop your Tenderstem broccoli into thirds





Step 3

Add the chopped potatoes to a pot with a lid with plenty of boiled water and bring to the boil over a high heat Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium, add your chopped broccoli to a sieve and place the sieve over the potatoes Cover with a lid and cook for 12-15 min or until the potatoes and broccoli are fork-tender





Step 4

Once the meatballs have browned, reduce the heat to medium-high and add the sliced onion Cook for 1-2 min or until starting to soften Peel and finely chop (or grate) your garlic





Step 5

Once starting to soften, add your onion marmalade with your gravy base, chopped garlic, tomato paste, cornflour and a generous pinch of salt Give everything a good mix up until the meatballs are well coated





Step 6

Once well coated, add 250ml [325ml] [425ml] cold water and bring to the boil over a high heat Once boiling, cover with a lid Cook for a final 10-11 min, stirring occasionally, or until the sauce has thickened and the meatballs are cooked through (no pink meat!) – these are your saucy onion gravy beef meatballs





Step 7

Once the potatoes are tender, set aside the steamed broccoli and drain the potatoes Return the drained potatoes to the pot with a small knob of butter, a splash of milk and a generous pinch of salt and pepper and mash roughly – these are your crushed potatoes



Step 8

Serve the saucy onion gravy beef meatballs with the crushed potatoes and steamed broccoli to the side Season with a pinch of pepper Enjoy!
