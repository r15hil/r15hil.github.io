Step 4

Step 5

Once the meatballs are browned, add your curry powder and tomato paste to the pan with a small knob of butter Cook for 1 min or until fragrant





Step 6

Once fragrant, add your beef stock mix to the pan with 175ml [230ml] [300ml] boiled water and a generous pinch of salt and pepper Give everything a good mix up Cook, covered, for 12-15 min or until cooked through (no pink meat!), stirring occasionally – this is your beef meatball curry





Step 7

Step 8

Serve the beef meatball & spinach curry with the cooked rice to the side Enjoy!
