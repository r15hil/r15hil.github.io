Smoky Halloumi & Carrot Wraps

Description: Super fast and easy to serve, these white flour tortilla wraps are home to pan-fried halloumi, carrot and cumin salad, lamb's lettuce and a smoky mayo. That's a wrap (a good one).

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 225g Cypriot halloumi
  • 6 white tortilla wraps
  • 50g lambs lettuce
  • 2 spring onions
  • 1 mayonnaise sachet (26ml)
  • 400g carrots


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle Preheat the oven to 180°C/ 160°C (fan)/ 350°F/ Gas 4 Top and tail the carrots and cut them into rough, bite-size pieces (no need to peel them!) 





Step 2

Add the carrots to a pot (with a matching lid) of boiled water over a high heat and bring to the boil Cover and cook for 10-15 min or until they're tender 





Step 3

Meanwhile, trim and chop the spring onions finely





Step 4

Place half of the smoked paprika in a bowl (save the rest for another time!) Add 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] boiled water and leave for 1 min (the heat helps the flavours of the paprika to develop!)  Add the mayonnaise with a pinch of salt and mix together - this is your smoky mayo





Step 5

Heat a large, dry, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) over a medium heat Once hot, toast the cumin seeds for 1-2 min or until they're fragrant, then transfer them to a plate and keep the pan for later  Pat the halloumi dry, and cut it into six slices 





Step 6

Drain the carrots and add them to a food processor  Add a knob of butter and the toasted cumin seeds  Season with pepper Blitz to a rough texture - this is your spiced carrot   





Step 7

Add the tortilla wraps to a baking tray and place it in the oven for 2-3 min or until the wraps are warmed through Tip: don't leave the wraps in the oven for too long or they will go crispy  Return the pan to a high heat and add the sliced halloumi Cook for 1-2 min on each side or until it's golden brown  Meanwhile, wash and dry the lamb's lettuce   



Step 8

Fill your wraps like this: spiced carrot, lamb's lettuce, halloumi, smoky mayo and top with the spring onion  Enjoy!
