Step 6

Step 7

Whilst the stew is cooking, wash your spinach Add the spinach to the pan and cook, covered, for 1-2 min or until the spinach is wilted



Step 8

Serve the warming Persian-style lean beef & lentil stew over the cooked rice Tip: Watch out for any cardamom pods! Crumble your feta over the top and season with a grind of black pepper Enjoy!
