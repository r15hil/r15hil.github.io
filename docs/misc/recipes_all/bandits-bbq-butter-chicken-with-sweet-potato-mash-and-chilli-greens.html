Whilst the chicken is in the oven, add the chopped sweet potato to a pot with plenty of boiled water and bring to the boil over a high heat Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium and cook for 12-15 min or until fork-tender, once done, drain and return to the pot to steam dry Reboil a kettle
