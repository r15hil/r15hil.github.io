Chipotle Fish, Green Rice & Cucumber Salsa

Description: In this speedy Mexican dish you'll grind fresh coriander to a paste and stir it through cooked basmati rice to make a flavour-packed, vividly coloured 'green rice'. Fiery chipotle fish sits on top, with a cooling lime, grated cucumber & tomato salsa on the side! (Gluten and dairy-free, suitable for coeliacs).

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 125g cherry tomatoes
  • 2 basa fillets
  • 130g basmati rice
  • 1 chipotle paste sachet (20g)
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 20g coriander
  • 1 lime


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 220°C/ 200°C (fan)/ 425°F/ Gas 7 Add the basmati rice and 300ml [600ml] cold water to a pot with a lid and bring to the boil over a high heat Once boiling, reduce the heat to very low and cook, covered, for 10-15 min or until all the water has absorbed and the rice is cooked Once cooked, remove from the heat and keep covered until serving





Step 2

Meanwhile, add the basa fillets to a lightly oiled baking tray  Spread the the chipotle paste (Can't handle the heat? Go easy!) over the basa and season with a pinch of salt Put the tray in the oven for 8-10 min or until cooked through 





Step 3

Meanwhile, chop the coriander finely, including the stalks Add the chopped coriander to a pestle and mortar with a pinch of salt and 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] cold water Grind the coriander to a fine paste and set aside 





Step 4

Cut the cucumber in half and scrape out the inner seeds with a teaspoon, throw the watery seeds away Grate the cucumber 





Step 5

Chop the cherry tomatoes roughly into small pieces   





Step 6

Add the chopped cherry tomatoes and cucumber to a bowl Add the juice of 1/2 [1] lime and season with a pinch of salt – this is your cucumber & tomato salsa





Step 7

Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and stir through the coriander paste Season with a pinch of salt – this is your green rice 



Step 8

Cut the remaining lime into wedges Serve the cooked fish over the green rice with the cucumber & tomato salsa to the side Garnish with a wedge of lime Enjoy!
