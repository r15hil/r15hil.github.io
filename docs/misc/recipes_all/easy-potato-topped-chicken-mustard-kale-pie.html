Easy Potato-Topped Chicken, Mustard & Kale Pie

Description: This potato-topped chicken casserole is as easy as pie to prep. You'll combine diced chicken, soft cheese and shredded kale in an oven-proof dish and top the lot with grated potato and cheese. Pop it in the oven to bubble away until it's crisp, golden and very tempting! (Gluten-free, suitable for coeliacs).

Ingredients

  • 40g mature cheddar
  • 100g soft cheese
  • 250g British diced chicken breast
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 120g shredded kale
  • 300g potatoes
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 200°C/ 180°C (fan)/ 425°F/ Gas 7 Boil a kettle  Dissolve the chicken stock cube[s] in 150ml [300ml] boiled water





Step 2

Whisk the Dijon mustard, cream cheese and cornflour with the chicken stock until smooth - this is your cooking sauce





Step 3

Add the kale, diced chicken and cooking sauce to a wide oven-proof dish and season with a pinch of salt and pepper  Give everything a good old mix up and set aside Tip: use individual pie dishes for fancy presentation





Step 4

Grate the cheddar cheese  Grate the potatoes (skins on) 





Step 5

Squeeze any excess water out of the grated potatoes  Tip: wrap the grated potatoes in a clean tea towel and twist the cloth to remove as much water as possible





Step 6

Mix the grated potatoes and cheddar cheese with a generous pinch of salt and a large drizzle of vegetable oil





Step 7

Top the chicken and kale mixture with the grated potatoes  Put the tray in the oven for 45-50 min or until golden and crispy and the chicken is cooked through– this is your potato-topped chicken, mustard & kale pie 



Step 8

Once done, remove the tray from the oven and leave the pie to cool slightly for a few min  Serve the potato-topped chicken, mustard & kale pie Enjoy!
