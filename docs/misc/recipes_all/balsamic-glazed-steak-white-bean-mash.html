Balsamic-Glazed Steak & White Bean Mash

Description: Balsamic creates a sweet, syrupy glaze for our steaks which gives them a tart, yet savoury depth. Served with white bean mash, a warm courgette and tomato side and scattered with fresh basil. This is steak and mash - Mediterranean style!

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato
  • 1 courgette
  • 1 tin of cannellini beans
  • 2 British minute steaks
  • 300g potatoes
  • 10g fresh basil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 4 tbsp balsamic vinegar


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle  Peel and cut the potatoes into rough, bite-size pieces (skins on) Peel the garlic Add the potatoes and whole garlic cloves to a pot (with a matching lid) of salted boiling water  





Step 2

Bring the pot to the boil over a high heat and cook, covered, for 10-15 min or until fork-tender  Once cooked, drain and return to the pot with a knob of butter and splash of milk, then cover and set aside until step 7  Tip: Covering the hot, cooked potatoes encourages them to steam and they will mash more easily





Step 3

Meanwhile, cut the courgette(s) into quarters lengthways, then into bite-size pieces Chop the tomato(es) into a large dice  





Step 4

Add 2-3 tbsp vegetable oil to a pan over a medium-high heat Once hot, add the courgette and a pinch of salt and cook for 3 min or until coloured Reduce the heat to medium-low, add the tomatoes with a pinch of sugar and cook for a further 5 min or until slightly softened, then remove from the heat and set aside  





Step 5

Pat the steaks dry with kitchen towel, rub both sides with vegetable oil and season both sides with salt Heat a separate pan over a medium-high heat





Step 6

Once the pan is very hot, add the steaks and cook for 1 min on one side only Flip the steak and add the balsamic vinegar with a pinch of sugar (this helps it caramelise)  Cook for 1 min further, then transfer the cooked steaks (with any remaining balsamic) to a plate and cover with a tea towel to 'rest' until serving





Step 7

Mash the potatoes until smooth and season very generously with salt and pepper Rinse and drain the cannellini beans and add them to the mash Place the pot over a medium-low heat and cook for 3 min or until the beans are warmed through



Step 8

Chop the basil finely, including the stalks  Serve the steak along with any resting juices and excess balsamic over the mash with the courgette and tomato to the side Scatter over the basil Enjoy!
