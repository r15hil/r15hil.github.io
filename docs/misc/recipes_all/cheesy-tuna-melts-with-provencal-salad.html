Cheesy Tuna Melts With Provençal Salad

Description: This cheesy tuna melt takes its inspiration from the classic French Salade Niçoise. You’ll pack tuna, cheddar cheese and a sweet onion marmalade into individual focaccia rolls and serve alongside a green bean, olive, lettuce and tomato salad. Who said salad supper has to be dull? Not moi!

Ingredients

  • 80g mature cheddar
  • 125g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 little gem lettuce
  • 80g trimmed fine green beans
  • 2 focaccia
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 20g onion marmalade
  • 2 mayonnaise sachets (30ml)
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tin of tuna (160g)
  • 30g pitted black olives
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 garlic clove


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle, then set some butter near a warm place to reach room temperature for easy spreading in step 3 Chop the green beans into bite-sized pieces Add the green beans to a pot of boiled water with a pinch of salt, bring to the boil over a high heat and cook for 5 min or until tender Once tender, drain and set aside 





Step 2

Meanwhile, grate the cheddar cheese  Drain the tuna  Add the drained tuna and the grated cheddar to a bowl with a generous pinch of salt and give everything a good mix up 





Step 3

Slice the focaccia in half Butter the middle and tops of the focaccia, then also smear the middle with onion marmalade Sandwich the cheese & tuna mix between two slices – these are your tuna sandwiches  





Step 4

Heat a large, non-stick pan over a medium heat and once hot, add the tuna sandwiches and cover with a smaller pan Add a heavy weight (eg. several tins) to the top pan and cook for 3-4 min per side, checking occasionally – these are your tuna melts





Step 5

Meanwhile, rip the little gem lettuce into bite-sized pieces Chop the black olives roughly Slice the cherry tomatoes in half Peel and slice the shallot[s] finely





Step 6

Peel and chop (or grate) the garlic finely  Combine the mayo, chopped garlic, apple cider vinegar, dried oregano and Dijon mustard with a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt and pepper – this is your Provençal dressing





Step 7

Add the drained green beans, chopped cherry tomatoes, olives, shallot, little gem lettuce and Provençal dressing to a large bowl and give everything a good old mix up – this is your Provençal salad 



Step 8

Serve the tuna melt with the Provençal salad to the side Enjoy!
