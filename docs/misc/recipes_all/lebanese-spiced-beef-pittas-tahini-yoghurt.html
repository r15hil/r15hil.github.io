Lebanese Spiced Beef Pittas & Tahini Yoghurt

Description: These Lebanese beef 'arayes' filled pittas are an easy yet adventurous treat the whole family can enjoy. You'll stuff the pittas with a flavoursome meat filling before baking, leaving you with crispy pockets of deliciousness. Serve with a fresh cucumber & tomato salad and cool tahini yoghurt. Ara-yes please!

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 4 wholemeal pittas
  • 1 red wine vinegar sachet (15ml)
  • 250g British beef mince
  • 1 tbsp ras el hanout
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 10g parsley
  • 120g natural yoghurt
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 garlic cloves


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 220°C/ 200°C (fan)/ 400°F/ Gas 6 and boil a kettle Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic, then chop the parsley finely (including the stalks) Dissolve the beef stock cube[s] in 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] boiled water, then add the beef mince, beef stock, garlic, half the parsley, ras el hanout and a pinch of black pepper and give everything a good mix up  





Step 2

Cut the pitta bread in half and carefully peel the pockets open Tip: don't worry if you tear the bread a little





Step 3

Divide the beef mince into 8 [16] and carefully fill each pitta pocket Close the pitta pocket and press down firmly – these are your beef arayes  





Step 4

Add the beef arayes to a baking tray and top with another baking tray Add a heavy weight (e.g an oven-proof dish) and put the trays in the oven for 15-18 min or until the beef is cooked through  Tip: the heavy weight flattens the arayes to help them stay together





Step 5

Meanwhile, combine the tahini, natural yoghurt and a drizzle of olive oil in a small bowl Season with a pinch of salt and pepper and give it a good mix up – this is your tahini yoghurt  





Step 6

Dice the tomatoes  Chop the cucumber roughly Peel and finely slice the shallot[s]





Step 7

Add the chopped tomato, cucumber and shallot to a bowl with the remaining chopped parsley, red wine vinegar and 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] olive oil  Season with a pinch of salt and pepper and give everything a good mix up – this is your cucumber & tomato salad 



Step 8

Slice the beef arayes in half lengthways Serve the beef arayes with the cucumber & tomato salad and tahini yoghurt to the side Enjoy!
