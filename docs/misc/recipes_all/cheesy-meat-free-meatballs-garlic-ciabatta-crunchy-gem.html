Instructions

Step 5

Step 6

Once the sauce has thickened, transfer the meatballs and sauce to an oven-proof dish (or individual dishes) Top with the torn mozzarella and put the dish in the oven for 10-12 min or until melted and bubbling – these are your cheesy meat-free meatballs Meanwhile, combine the remaining balsamic vinegar with 2 tbsp [3 tbsp] [4 tbsp] olive oil and some pepper – this is your balsamic dressing
