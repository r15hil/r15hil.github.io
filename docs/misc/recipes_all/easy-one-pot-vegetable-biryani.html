Easy One-Pot Plant-Based Vegetable Biryani

Description: Traditionally, biryanis are baked for hours, but this plant-based version is a one-pot timesaver. You'll fill it to the brim with cauliflower, green beans and spices, and serve with a dollop of cooling cultured coconut. Yum!
