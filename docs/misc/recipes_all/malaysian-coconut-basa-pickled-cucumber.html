Malaysian-Style Coconut Basa With Rice

Description: Inspired by the Malaysian classic, ayam percik, this pescatarian take on the fragrant curried dish is a feast for the senses. It’s loaded with lemongrass, chilli and aromats and served with basmati rice and quick-pickled cucumber.
