Squash & Wheat Berry Salad

Description: Wheatberries are a high fibre, wholegrain. They retain their shape really well and have a nutty flavour, so they're perfect in salads. This roasted butternut squash, red onion, feta and rocket salad is the perfect example of a salad with real bite, topped with mixed seeds.

Ingredients

  • 100g Greek Feta Cheese Cypressa
  • 30g 3 seed mix
  • 1 tbsp dried sage
  • 130g wheat berries
  • 50g rocket salad
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 red onion


Instructions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 200°C/ 180°C (fan)/ 400°F/ Gas 6 Boil a kettle  Add the butternut squash to a baking tray (use tin foil to avoid mess!), drizzle with olive oil and season with both salt and pepper and give it a good mix  Place the tray in the oven and cook for 20-30 min or until the squash is tender and lightly browned   



Step 2

Meanwhile, add the wheat berries to a pot with plenty of boiled water and a pinch of salt (the same as you would for pasta) Bring back to the boil, then cook over a high heat for 12-15 min or until they're tender with a slight bite Once cooked, drain and return to the pot and leave to cool       





Step 3

Meanwhile, peel the red onion[s] and cut into wedges Once the squash has been in the oven for 15 min, add the onion and dried sage to the tray, then return it to the oven for the remaining cooking time   



Step 4

Meanwhile, peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic Juice the lemon[s] Mix the chopped garlic and lemon juice with 2 tbsp [4 tbsp] olive oil and season with salt and pepper - this is your lemon dressing





Step 5

Wash and dry the rocket salad  Scatter the 3 seed mix all over the squash and onion 5 mins before they're done





Step 6

Crumble the feta  Wash and dry the rocket salad





Step 7

Once the squash and onions are done, add the and cooked wheat berries to the baking tray and give the lot a good mix  Gently tumble the rocket leaves and lemon dressing through 



Step 8

Serve and enjoy!
