Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 200°C/ 180°C (fan)/ gas 6 Boil a full kettle Peel and finely chop (or grate) your garlic Peel and finely dice half you brown onion, then finely slice the remaining onion





Step 2

Add your Fable pulled mushrooms to an oven-proof dish Sprinkle over your ground cumin and ground smoked paprika with 2/3 of your cider vinegar and half your agave (you'll use the rest later!) Pour in 100ml [130ml] [180ml] boiled water with half your vegetable stock mix (save the rest for later!) and give everything a good mix up, then put the dish in the oven for 25 min or until most of the liquid has been absorbed and the mushrooms are slightly crispy





Step 3
