Zesty Mozzarella & Spiced Couscous

Description: Spice up your couscous (life) with this cherry tomato, sultana, turmeric and baharat (aka 7 spice) tumble. Served with a fresh, milky seasoned mozzarella with lime and mint. Perfect with a chilled glass of white wine!

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 10g fresh mint
  • 1 tbsp baharat
  • 1 tsp bright yellow turmeric
  • 250g fresh mozzarella
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag of cashew nuts
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 120g golden couscous
  • 1 lime
  • 30g sultanas


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle Crush or chop the cashews coarsely Tip: Bash the nuts in their bag with a rolling pin for less mess Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic    





Step 2

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a matching lid with 1 tbsp (2 tbsp) olive oil over a medium heat Once hot, add the cashew nuts, garlic, sultanas, baharat and turmeric with a large pinch of salt and cook for 1 min, stirring constantly





Step 3

Add the couscous and stir for 30 seconds to coat in spice Cover with 200ml (400ml) boiled water and bring to the boil over a high heat Once boiling, cover with a lid and remove from the heat  Set aside covered until serving and the water has absorbed





Step 4

Meanwhile, cut the cherry tomatoes in half Strip the mint leaves from their stalks and chop finely





Step 5

Drain the mozzarella and tear into rough bite-size pieces  





Step 6

Zest (be careful not to include any bitter white pith) the lime(s) over the torn mozzarella, drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper Cut the zested lime(s) in half





Step 7

Fluff the cooked couscous with a fork  Season very generously with salt and pepper  Stir through the cherry tomatoes and half the mint Squeeze over the juice of half the (whole) lime



Step 8

Mix the mozzarella through the couscous and garnish with the remaining mint and lime (cut into wedges) Enjoy!
