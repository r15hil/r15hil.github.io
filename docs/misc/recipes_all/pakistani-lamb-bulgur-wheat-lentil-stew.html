Pakistani Lamb, Bulgur Wheat & Lentil Stew

Description: In this recipe, we've taken inspiration from the famous Pakistani dish 'haleem', which is often eaten during Eid. With slow-cooked lamb mince, bulgur wheat and red lentils, it's a comforting meal infused with warming spices like turmeric and garam masala. Serve topped with lemon, herbs and toasted cashews for crunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 250g British lamb mince

  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 green chilli
  • 10g coriander & mint mix
  • 25g cashew nuts
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 brown onions
  • 1 lemon
  • 75g bulgur wheat
  • 100g red lentils
