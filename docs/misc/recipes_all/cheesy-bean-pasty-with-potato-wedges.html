Cheesy Bean Pasty With Potato Wedges

Description: This cheesy pasty is a comforting classic. You’ll simmer cannellini beans in a sweet-savoury sauce before loading them into flaky pastry parcels and topping with cheese. Bake till melty and serve with potato wedges to finish.
