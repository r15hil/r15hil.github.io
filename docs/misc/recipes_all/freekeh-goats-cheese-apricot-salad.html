Freekeh, Goats' Cheese & Apricot Salad

Description: This simple, wholesome freekeh salad marries soft mild goats' cheese with roast red onion 'petals', apricots, toasted cashews and fresh basil. A tumble of goodness.

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 50g diced apricots
  • 130g freekeh
  • 10g basil
  • 1 bag of cashew nuts
  • 125g soft goats' cheese
  • 1 red onion


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 220°C/ 200°C (fan)/ 425°F/ Gas 7 Rinse the freekeh, then add it to a pot with 500ml [1L] water and bring to the boil over a high heat Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium and cook for 20 min or until tender but with a slight bite





Step 2

Chop the red onion[s] into wedges, keeping the peel on Place on a baking tray and sprinkle with a pinch of salt Place the tray in the oven for 25 min or until they are tender





Step 3

Meanwhile, add the cashew nuts to a separate baking dish Put it in the oven for 6 min or until the nuts have turned a darker golden colour Once golden, remove them from the oven and leave them to cool





Step 4

Meanwhile, add the diced apricots to a large mixing bowl with the red wine vinegar and 2 tbsp [4 tbsp] olive oil (preferably extra virgin) Season generously with salt and pepper, mix well and set aside – this is your apricot dressing





Step 5

Once the freekeh is tender, drain it and allow to steam dry in a sieve for a few min





Step 6

Once the onions are tender, remove them from the oven and set aside to cool slightly Meanwhile, chop the basil finely, including the stalks Chop the cooled cashew nuts coarsely 





Step 7

Remove the roots and skins from the cooled onion and separate them into ‘petals’ Add the drained freekeh to the apricot dressing, along with the chopped cashews, basil and onion petals  Mix gently and taste for seasoning, adding more salt and pepper if needed – this is your mixed freekeh salad



Step 8

Serve the mixed freekeh salad with the soft goats' cheese broken into bite-sized pieces over the top Enjoy!
