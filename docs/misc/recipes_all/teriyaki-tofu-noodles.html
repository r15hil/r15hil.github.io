Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 200g tofu
  • 100g runner beans
  • 10g toasted sesame seeds
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 15g root ginger
  • 3 soy sauce sachets (24ml)
  • 1 lime
  • 3 wheat noodle nests (150g)


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle Peel the ginger (scrape the skin off with a teaspoon) and finely chop (or grate) Zest and juice the lime(s) Combine the ginger, zest and lime juice in a bowl





Step 2

Add the soy sauce, sesame oil, 2 tbsp (4 tbsp) sugar (preferably brown) and the chilli flakes (can't handle the heat? Go easy!) Mix well- this is your teryaki sauce





Step 3

Remove the tofu from its packaging and pat dry with kitchen paper Cut in half and cut each half into 9 cubes Trim the beans and slice them finely





Step 4

Add the sesame seeds and the tofu to a sandwich bag Shake the bag so that the sesame seeds stick to the tofu Tip: If you don't have a sandwich bag, just add them to a bowl and toss with your hands





Step 5

Add the noodles and the beans to a large pot of salted boiling water  Cook for 7-9 min over a medium-high heat Once cooked, drain and rinse under a little cold water (this prevents the noodles sticking together)





Step 6

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with 2-3 tbsp vegetable oil Once hot, add the tofu and cook over a medium-high heat for 8-10 min, turning occasionally for even golden colouring Once golden, transfer the tofu to kitchen paper and season generously with salt and pepper





Step 7

Return the pan to a medium heat Add the teryaki sauce and whisk for approx. 2-3 min until it's reduced by half into a glaze



Step 8

Serve the noodles and beans with the tofu and teriyaki glaze spooned over Enjoy!
