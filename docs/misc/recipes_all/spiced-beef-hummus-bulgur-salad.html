Spiced Beef, Hummus & Bulgur Salad

Description: This is one of our favourite speedy dishes, which is typical to the Middle East. Homemade creamy hummus is topped with caramelised, spiced ground beef. Served with a fresh tabbouleh-style bulgur wheat salad. Comforting and healthy. Hum'must try!

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato
  • 250g British beef mince
  • 150g pilavlik bulgur
  • 1 tbsp ras el hanout
  • 1 tin of chickpeas
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 10g fresh parsley
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tbsp tahini paste
  • 30g sultanas
  • 1 lemon


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle Add the bulgur wheat to a pot with plenty of boiled water and a pinch of salt (the same as you would for pasta) and cook over a high heat for 7-10 min or until tender with a slight bite Once tender, drain and set aside in a sieve to cool





Step 2

Cut the lemon[s] in half Peel and chop the garlic roughly Meanwhile, drain and rinse the chickpeas and add them to a food processor





Step 3

Add the tahini paste, chopped garlic, 50ml [100ml] olive oil, a big splash of water and 1 tsp [2 tsp] salt to your food processor Juice half of the lemon[s] into the food processor Blitz until smooth and creamy then set aside - this is your homemade hummus 





Step 4

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with 1/2 tbsp [1 tbsp] vegetable oil over a high heat Once hot, add the beef mince and the ras el hanout and cook for 3 min or until starting to brown, breaking it up as you go Once browned, add the sultanas and cook for a further 2 min  Season generously with salt and pepper and set aside - this is your spiced beef 





Step 5

Meanwhile, dice the cucumber finely Dice the tomato[es] roughly Trim, then slice the spring onion[s] finely Chop the parsley finely, including the stalks  





Step 6

Juice the remaining lemon into a large bowl  Add 2 tbsp [4 tbsp] olive oil and a generous pinch of salt and pepper - this is your lemon dressing 





Step 7

Add the cooked bulgur wheat, diced tomato, diced cucumber, sliced spring onion and chopped parsley to a mixing bowl Add the lemon dressing and give everything a good mix - this is your bulgur wheat salad



Step 8

Serve the spiced beef over the homemade hummus with the bulgur wheat salad to the side Enjoy!
