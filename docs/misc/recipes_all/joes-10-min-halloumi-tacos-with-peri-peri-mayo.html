Ingredients

  • 150g canned sweetcorn
  • 30ml mayonnaise
  • 200g halloumi
  • 6 plain tortillas
  • 15ml cider vinegar
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 spring onions
  • 1 lime

Step 3

Step 4

Add the drained sweetcorn and chopped spring onion to a bowl Add the juice of the lime half[ves] (save the wedges for later!) and a pinch of salt, give it a good mix up – this is your sweetcorn salsa





Step 5

Cut the avocado[s] in half lengthways, around the stone[s] Remove the stone[s] using a teaspoon Scoop the avocado out of the skin[s] using a spoon and slice it finely, lengthways
