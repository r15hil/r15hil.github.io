Cajun Chicken With Apricot Couscous

Description: This super simple chicken dish is awash with colour thanks to yellow apricots, pretty onion ‘petals’, vivid green beans and red pepper. We’ve supplied you with all the spices you need to make up your own DIY Cajun seasoning – equal amounts of smoked paprika, ground cumin and ground coriander...all you need is plenty of black pepper!

Ingredients

  • 120g trimmed fine green beans
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 150g Greek-style yoghurt
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 50g diced apricots
  • 1 tsp ground smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 British chicken breast fillet
  • 120g couscous
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 red onion


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 220°C/ 200°C (fan)/ 425°F/ Gas 7 Chop the red onion[s] into wedges, keeping the peel on (this helps it cook quicker) Add the onion wedges to a baking tray, drizzle with a little vegetable oil and season them with a pinch of salt Put the tray in the oven for 15-20 min or until they're tender





Step 2

Meanwhile, combine the ground cumin, ground coriander, smoked paprika, dried oregano and 1 tsp [2 tsp] black pepper and a very large pinch of salt on a plate Mix all the spices together – this is your Cajun seasoning 





Step 3

Boil a kettle Cut the chicken breast[s] into strips Add the chicken strips to your Cajun seasoning and make sure they're fully coated in the spices   





Step 4

Dissolve the chicken stock cube[s] in 200ml [400ml] boiled water in a large heatproof bowl  Add the couscous and diced apricots to the bowl, cover with a tea towel or plate and set aside – this is your apricot couscous Deseed the red pepper[s] (scrape the seeds and pith out with a teaspoon) and cut into bite-sized pieces 





Step 5

Cut the green beans in half  Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a matching lid, with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium-high heat Once hot, add the Cajun spiced chicken, green beans and red pepper and cook for 3 min, turning once halfway through Add a splash of water to the pan, cover with a lid and cook for a further 2 min





Step 6

Meanwhile, season the Greek-style yoghurt with salt and pepper, add a drizzle of olive oil and mix well – this is your seasoned yoghurt Remove the roasted onion wedges from the oven and carefully remove the skins by cutting off the root ends – you'll end up with onion 'petals'  





Step 7

Once the chicken is cooked, remove the pan from the heat, add the red wine vinegar and mix to combine Fluff the apricot couscous with a fork Carefully combine the cooked Cajun chicken, green beans and red pepper with the fluffed apricot couscous and onion petals 



Step 8

Serve the Cajun Chicken Couscous with a dollop of the seasoned yoghurt  Enjoy!
