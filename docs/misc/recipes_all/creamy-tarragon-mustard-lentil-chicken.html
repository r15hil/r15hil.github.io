French-Style Mustard & Garlic Chicken With Rice

Description: Who knew this classic French meal could be ready in minutes? You'll cook diced chicken in a creamy garlic and wholegrain mustard sauce with spinach and lentils, and serve with brown rice. Ingredients

  • Crème fraîche (80g)
  • British diced chicken thigh (250g)
  • Dried thyme (0.5tsp)
  • Cornflour (1tsp)
  • Green lentils (185g)
  • Cooked brown long grain rice (280g)
  • Chicken stock mix (11g)
  • Roasted garlic paste (15g)
  • Chicken stock mix (5.5g) x0
  • Wholegrain mustard (21g)
  • Spinach (80g)


Instructions



Step 1

Before you begin... Get all your ingredients and equipment ready Skim through these steps, then wash your fruit and veg Take your chicken out of the fridge, open the packet and let it air Note: Equipment and pan sizes vary. Make sure you cook food thoroughly (even if it takes you a little longer than 10 min)





Step 2

Now, let's get started! Boil a kettle Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a high heat Once hot, add the diced chicken thigh with a generous pinch of salt and cook for 4-5 min or until starting to brown





Step 3

Meanwhile, dissolve your chicken stock mix and cornflour in 150ml [195ml] [250ml] boiled water, with your wholegrain mustard (not a mustard fan? Go easy!) and crème fraîche – this is your creamy stock Wash your spinach then add to a colander and pour boiled water all over it so that it starts to wilt Rinse the wilted spinach under cold running water until it's cool Once cool, squeeze as much water out of the spinach as you can





Step 4

Drain and rinse your green lentils





Step 5

Once the chicken has browned, add your dried thyme and roasted garlic paste to the pan and cook for 30 secs or until fragrant Once fragrant, add the creamy stock to the pan with a pinch of salt and bring to the boil Season with a very generous grind of black pepper and cook for 3-4 min or until thickened





Step 6

Squeeze the pouch[es] of cooked brown long grain rice to separate the grains Tear the top corner of the pouch[es] (just a little!) and microwave for 2 min or until piping hot Tip: If you're cooking two pouches, pop them in together but increase the microwave cook time accordingly





Step 7

Once the sauce has thickened, add the drained lentils and spinach to the pan and cook for a further 3 min or until the chicken is cooked through (no pink meat!) – this is your French-style mustard & garlic chicken



Step 8

Serve the French-style mustard & garlic chicken over the cooked brown rice Enjoy!
