Lentil Kofta, Harissa & Feta Wraps

Description: We promise you won't miss the meat in this speedy vegetarian makeover of a Middle Eastern classic. Mashed lentils, spiced with ras el hanout, make the base of the kofta, which is smothered in a harissa mayo, topped with feta cheese and then all wrapped up in a cosy blanket of tortilla. That's a wrap!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp harissa paste
  • 1 little gem lettuce
  • 100g Greek Feta Cheese Cypressa
  • 1 tin of lentils
  • 2 mayonnaise sachets (30ml)
  • 1 tbsp ras el hanout
  • 1 British free-range egg
  • 60g panko breadrcrumbs
  • 6 white tortilla wraps
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 garlic clove


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 180°C/ 160°C (fan)/ 350°F/ Gas 4 Drain the tinned lentils





Step 2

Peel and chop (or grate) the garlic Mash the drained lentils in a flat-bottomed bowl (a pot is perfect!) to form a smooth paste Add the ras el hanout, chopped garlic, panko breadcrumbs, crack in the egg and add a generous pinch of salt Mix well until the breadcrumbs are fully combined – this is your kofta mixture





Step 3

Divide the kofta mixture into 6 [12] and shape into balls Press each ball into a sausage shape, and then flatten into a rectangular patty – these are your koftas





Step 4

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a drizzle of olive oil over a medium-high heat  Once hot, add the koftas and cook for 2 min on each side, or until they're crisp





Step 5

Meanwhile, shred the little gem lettuce Peel and slice the shallot[s]





Step 6

Combine the mayonnaise and harissa paste (Can't handle the heat? Go easy!) in a bowl Add a drizzle of olive oil and mix well – this is your harissa mayo 





Step 7

Add the tortilla wraps to a baking tray and place them in the oven for 2-3 min until they're warmed through



Step 8

Assemble the wraps like this: wrap, shredded lettuce, kofta, harissa mayo, crumbled feta and sliced shallot Enjoy!
