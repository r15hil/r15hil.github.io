Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 220°C/ 200°C (fan)/ gas 7, then take your chicken out of the fridge, open the packet and let it air Peel and finely dice your brown onion[s] Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a generous drizzle of vegetable oil over a high heat Once hot, add the diced onion and cook for 5-6 min or until softened and lightly browned

Meanwhile, dissolve your chicken stock mix in 150ml [225ml] [300ml] boiled water Add your sultanas and stir it all together – this is your sultana stock

Step 8

Serve the coronation chicken thigh pie topped with a pie lid and the buttery veg to the side Season with black pepper Enjoy!
