Step 3

Meanwhile, add your fresh orecchiette to a pot of boiled water with a large pinch of salt and bring to the boil over a high heat Cook for 4-6 min or until cooked with a slight bite Once done, drain the orecchiette, reserving a cup of the starchy pasta water





Step 4

Once the sausage is browned, crumble in your chestnut mushrooms using your hands and cook for a further 2-3 min or until beginning to soften





Step 5

Peel and chop (or grate) your garlic Reduce the heat to medium-high and add the chopped garlic to the sausage and cook for 1 min or until fragrant





Step 6
