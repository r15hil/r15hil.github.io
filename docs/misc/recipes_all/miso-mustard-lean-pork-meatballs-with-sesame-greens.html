Instructions



Step 1

Step 2

Step 3

Divide your lean pork mixture and shape into 3 meatballs per person Add the lean pork meatballs to a baking tray with a drizzle of vegetable oil Put they tray in the oven for 15 min or until cooked through (no pink meat!)

Once beginning to soften, add the spinach and cook for 1-2 min further or until wilted – these are your sesame greens
