Ingredients

  • 1 red chilli
  • 2 pangasius fillets
  • 130g brown rice
  • 1 tsp 5 spice mix
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 spring onion
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
