Simple Crab, Chilli & Lemon Spaghetti

Description: This simple and sophisticated spaghetti is sure to impress. You'll make a silky sauce from olive oil, lemon, chilli and garlic to toss through spaghetti, asparagus and crab (the star of the show!) Full of fresh summer flavours, just let the ingredients do the talking! (Dairy-free: see our FAQs for details.)

Ingredients

  • 1 red chilli
  • 190g spaghetti
  • 120g asparagus spears
  • 1 Chinese rice wine sachet (15ml)
  • 100g Cornish fifty fifty crab
  • 10g parsley
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 lemon


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle Trim the asparagus, discarding the woody ends Chop the asparagus tips away from the stalks and set aside Roughly chop the stalks





Step 2

Add the spaghetti to a pot of boiled water with a generous pinch of salt and bring to the boil over a high heat Once boiling, cook for an initial 4-5 min  After 4-5 min add the chopped asparagus and cook for a further 4-5 min or until tender with a slight bite Once cooked, drain and reserve a cup of the starchy cooking water





Step 3

While the pasta is cooking, cut the red chilli[es] in half lengthways, deseed (scrape the seeds out with a teaspoon) and chop ﬁnely Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic Separate the white crab meat from the brown crab meat with a spoon (you’ll use the white meat at the end!)





Step 4

Chop the lemon[s] in half  Chop the parsley finely, including the stalks





Step 5

Heat a large pot over a low heat with 3 tbsp [6 tbsp] olive oil  Once hot, add the chopped chilli (can't handle the heat? Go easy!) and garlic and cook for 3-4 min until starting to soften Add the Chinese rice wine and give everything a good mix up Add the brown crab meat with a generous pinch of salt and mix to combine – this is your crab sauce





Step 6

Add the drained spaghetti to the crab sauce with 100ml [200ml] starchy pasta water and the juice of 1/2 [1] lemon  Give everything a good mix up until the sauce begins to stick to the pasta Tip: Add a splash more starchy pasta water if your sauce is looking a little dry!





Step 7

Remove the pot from the heat and gently stir through the white crab meat and chopped parsley – this is your crab, chilli & lemon spaghetti Chop the remaining lemon into wedges



Step 8

Serve the crab, chilli & lemon spaghetti with a lemon wedge to the side Season with a good grind of black pepper Enjoy!
