Herby Pork Steaks With Pear Slaw

Description: Pork partners really well with fruit, in this case fresh pear. You'll use it to make a slaw combined with carrot, celery and nigella seeds, served alongside oregano-crusted pork steaks and chunky chips.

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 2 British pork loin steaks
  • 1 tbsp Italian dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 pear
  • 300g potatoes
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 tsp black nigella seeds
  • 2 mayonnaise sachets (52ml)
  • 1 celery stick
  • 200g carrots


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 240°C/ 220°C (fan)/ 475°F/ Gas 9 Cut the potatoes (skins on) lengthways into chunky chips Place them on a baking tray, drizzle with vegetable oil and season generously with salt





Step 2

Place the tray in the oven for 25-30 min or until the chips are golden, turning once halfway Meanwhile, add the mayonnaise, cider vinegar, 2 tbsp (4 tbsp) olive oil and a generous pinch of salt and pepper to a large mixing bowl and mix well





Step 3

Peel and grate the carrots, discarding the root ends Grate the celery, discarding any tough fibrous bits Grate the pear, discarding the inner core Slice the spring onion(s) finely, discarding the root ends





Step 4

Combine the grated carrot, celery, pear and spring onion with the mayonnaise dressing and nigella seeds  Set aside until serving 





Step 5

Pat the pork steaks dry with kitchen towel Trim off the excess fat (optional) Rub them with 1 tbsp (2 tbsp) vegetable oil and coat them evenly in the dried oregano Season both sides generously with salt





Step 6

Heat a wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) over a high heat Once hot, add the pork steaks and cook for 2-3 min on each side, until browned on the outside and cooked through





Step 7

Transfer the cooked pork steaks to a plate and cover with a clean tea towel to rest  Leave them to rest for 5 min Tip: Covering with a teatowel keeps the meat warm without steaming it



Step 8

Serve the pork steaks with any resting juices spooned over and the slaw and chips to the side Enjoy!
