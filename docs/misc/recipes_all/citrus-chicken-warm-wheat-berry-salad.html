Citrus Chicken & Warm Wheat Berry Salad

Description: This zesty lemon chicken dish is served with a simple wheat berry salad packed with crisp green beans, crunchy radishes and warm baby plum tomatoes. You'll make a salty lemon caper butter dressing to toss it all in and top it with the sliced citrus chicken. It's simple, fresh and stylish, perfect for a summer's eve!

Ingredients

  • 80g fine green beans
  • 10g parsley & chive mix
  • 125g radishes
  • 125g baby plum tomatoes
  • 130g wheat berries
  • 1 large British chicken breast fillet
  • 15g caper buds
  • 4 spring onions
  • 1 lemon


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle Add the wheat berries to a large pot with plenty of boiled water and a pinch of salt (the same as you would for pasta) Cook over a high heat for 10 min





Step 2

Meanwhile, chop the baby plum tomatoes in half Slice the radishes finely into rounds Chop the green beans in half Trim, then finely slice the spring onions   





Step 3

Once the wheat berries have been cooking for 10 min, add the chopped green beans and cook for a further 3-4 min or until everything is tender  When the beans are tender, drain and return to the pot Add the chopped tomatoes, radishes and spring onions to the drained wheat berries and set aside 





Step 4

Meanwhile, zest the lemon[s] into a bowl Place your hand flat onto the chicken breast[s] Slice in half as if you were cutting a burger bun, so you are left with two thinner pieces Add the sliced chicken to the bowl of lemon zest with a generous drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt and pepper  Give everything a good old mix up





Step 5

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) over a medium-high heat and once hot, add the chicken to the pan with any remaining oil and lemon zest Cook for 6-8 min, turning once halfway or until cooked through – this is your citrus chicken Meanwhile, chop the capers finely Chop the chives and parsley finely, including the stalks   





Step 6

Once the chicken is cooked through, transfer it to a board to rest Return the pan to a very low heat and add 40g [80g] butter  Cook for 30 sec-1 min or until melted  Add the chopped capers and the juice of 1/2 [1] lemon and a pinch of salt – this is your caper butter dressing 





Step 7

Slice the rested chicken Add the caper butter dressing to the wheat berries with the chopped herbs and a very generous pinch of salt and pepper  Give everything a good mix up – this is your wheat berry salad 



Step 8

Cut the remaining lemon into wedges  Serve the sliced citrus chicken over the wheat berry salad  Season with a generous grind of black pepper and garnish with a lemon wedge Enjoy!
