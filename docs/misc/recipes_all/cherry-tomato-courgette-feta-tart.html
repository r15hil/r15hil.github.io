Cherry Tomato, Courgette & Feta Tart

Description: This classic flavour combination shouts summer supper. Fresh mint, courgette ribbons, salty feta and juicy tomatoes transform a simple tart into a seductive star. You’ll slather the pastry with a creamy garlic base for added punch, and serve with a dressed lamb’s lettuce salad. Tonight, let’s go al fresco!

Ingredients

  • 1 courgette
  • 5g mint
  • 325g all butter puff pastry
  • 1 balsamic vinegar sachet (15ml)
  • 50g lamb's lettuce
  • 100g Greek Feta Cheese Cypressa
  • 1 pot of crème fraîche (200g)
  • 1 spring onion
  • 125g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 220°C/ 200°C (fan)/ 425°F/ Gas 7 Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic  Peel lengths of courgette[s] until you end up with a pile of courgette 'ribbons' Cut the cherry tomatoes in half  Trim then finely slice the spring onion[s]





Step 2

Combine the chopped garlic with the crème fraîche and a pinch of salt – this is your creamy sauce





Step 3

Line a baking tray with non-stick baking paper Unwrap the puff pastry and cut it in half  Lay the pastry halves onto the lined baking tray Score a 1cm border around the edge of the pastry with a knife Tip: the border will puff up to give you a nice crust!





Step 4

Spread the creamy sauce over the puff pastry within the border Top the creamy sauce with the courgette ribbons, chopped cherry tomatoes and sliced spring onion Crumble over the feta cheese  Season with generous grind of black pepper





Step 5

Put the tray in the oven and cook for 35-40 min or until the pastry is cooked through, puffed up and golden – this is your courgette & cherry tomato tart





Step 6

Meanwhile, strip the mint leaves from their stems and chop them roughly





Step 7

Combine the balsamic vinegar with 2 tbsp [4 tbsp] olive oil and a pinch of salt in a small bowl – this is your balsamic dressing



Step 8

Dress the lamb's lettuce with your balsamic dressing Serve the courgette & cherry tomato tart with the dressed lamb's lettuce to the side Garnish with the chopped mint Enjoy!
