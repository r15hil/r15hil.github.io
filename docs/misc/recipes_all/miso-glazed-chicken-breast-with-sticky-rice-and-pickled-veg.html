Instructions



Step 1

Keep the lid on the whole time to get fluffy and sticky rice!

Step 5

Add your white miso paste, honey, ginger & garlic paste and mirin to a bowl with 1/2 tbsp [3/4 tbsp] [1 tbsp] boiled water and stir it all together – this is your miso glaze





Step 6
