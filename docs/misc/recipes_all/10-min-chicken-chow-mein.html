Chicken & Pepper Chow Mein

Description: Get this takeaway classic on the table in 10 minutes. You'll toss noodles with chicken, Romano pepper and mangetout, and coat with a homemade version of the famous sticky sauce. Sizzle, stir, done!

Ingredients

  • Toasted sesame oil (10ml)
  • Hoisin sauce (40g)
  • British diced chicken breast (125g) x0
  • Medium egg noodle nests (2pcs)
  • British diced chicken breast (250g)
  • Mangetout (80g)
  • Soy sauce (30ml)
  • Soy sauce (15ml) x0
  • Sweet pointed pepper
  • Hoisin sauce (20g) x0


Instructions



Step 1

Before you begin... Get all your ingredients and equipment ready Skim through these steps, then wash your fruit and veg Take your chicken out of the fridge, open the packet and let it air Note: Equipment and pan sizes vary. Make sure you cook food thoroughly (even if it takes you a little longer than 10 min)





Step 2

Now, let's get started! Boil a kettle Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a high heat Deseed your sweet pointed pepper[s] (scrape the seeds and pith out with a teaspoon), then chop roughly into rounds





Step 3

Once the pan is hot, add your diced chicken breast and chopped pepper and cook for 4-5 min or until the chicken is starting to brown





Step 4

Meanwhile, add your medium egg noodles to a pot and cover with boiled water until fully submerged Bring to the boil over a high heat and cook for 5-6 min or until cooked with a slight bite Once done, drain and set aside





Step 5

Once the chicken has browned, add your mangetout and cook for 4-5 min further





Step 6

Add your soy sauce, hoisin sauce and toasted sesame oil to the pan and cook for 2 min or until the chicken is cooked through (no pink meat!) Note: Please make sure you cook your chicken thoroughly (until piping hot!) as cooking equipment varies





Step 7

Add the drained noodles and give everything a good mix up – this is your chicken & pepper chow mein



Step 8

Serve the chicken & pepper chow mein Enjoy!
