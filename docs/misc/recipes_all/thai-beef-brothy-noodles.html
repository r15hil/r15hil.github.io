Thai Beef & Brothy Noodles

Description: This Thai-inspired bowl of broth combines fresh vegetables, Thai noodles and aromas that will comfort and soothe.

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 300g British beef mince
  • 100g carrots
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 100g sugar snap peas
  • 1 tbsp Thai red curry paste
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 30g root ginger
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 celery stick
  • 100g Thai rice noodles


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a full kettle Peel the ginger (scrape the skin off with a teaspoon) and finely chop (or grate) Combine the beef mince, Thai red curry paste and ginger in a bowl





Step 2

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) over a high heat Once hot, add the beef mixture and cook for 4 min or until cooked through (it will no longer be pink) Once cooked, remove from the heat





Step 3

Meanwhile, add 1L (2L) boiled water to a pot over a medium-high heat Add the beef stock cube(s), hoisin sauce, tomato paste and a big pinch of salt Stir to combine - this is your broth Keep the broth on a medium high heat until step 6 





Step 4

Meanwhile, slice the celery finely  Peel, halve the carrots lengthways and slice finely, discarding the root ends Slice the sugar snap peas finely  Tip: you can gather and slice several peas at a time





Step 5

Break the Thai rice noodles in half and add them to the broth Cover and cook for 4 min





Step 6

Add the carrot and celery to the pot, cover and cook for a further 2 min Add half of the sugar snap peas and remove the pot from the heat  Tip: adding the peas last minute ensures they stay crunchy!





Step 7

Add the cooked beef to the pot Stir to combine



Step 8

Divide the beef and brothy noodles between your bowls and garnish with the remaining raw sugar snap peas Tip: Serve with a spoon AND a fork Enjoy!
