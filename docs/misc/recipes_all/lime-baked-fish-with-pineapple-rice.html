Lime Baked Fish With Pineapple Rice

Description: You'll bake these basa fillets with a coriander-lime crust and serve it with spiced pineapple rice. Sweet, citrussy and herby - we feel like we're on a Caribbean holiday. It's time to bake that basa!

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 2 basa fillets
  • 130g brown rice
  • 10g fresh coriander
  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 15g root ginger
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tin of pineapple


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 180°C/ 160°C (fan)/ 400°F/ Gas 6 Add the rice, coriander seeds and a large pinch of salt to a medium pot over a high heat with 500ml [1L] cold water Bring to the boil and cook for 25-30 min or until tender with a slight bite Drain and return to the pot, covered, until later





Step 2

Meanwhile, zest the lime[s] then cut 4 [8] thin slices from the centre[s] and reserve the remaining ends for garnish  Peel the ginger (scrape the skin off with a teaspoon) and finely chop (or grate) Chop the bottom half of the coriander (mainly stalks) finely, saving the leaves for a later step





Step 3

Grind the lime zest, ginger, half the coriander, a pinch of salt and pepper and 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] vegetable oil in a pestle and mortar to form a paste Tip: If you don't have a pestle and mortar, chop everything very finely and mix with vegetable oil





Step 4

Place the fish onto a lightly oiled baking tray (use tin foil to avoid a mess) Spread the lime/ginger paste over the fish Top each fillet with 2 lime slices  





Step 5

Cook the fish on the top rack for 8-10 min or until the lime slices are beginning to brown Tip: Your fish is cooked when it turns opaque and flakes easily





Step 6

Meanwhile, chop the pineapple finely Chop the remaining coriander (mainly leaves) roughly





Step 7

Once the rice has been cooked and drained, add the pineapple and the remaining coriander Stir to combine Taste for seasoning adding more salt and pepper if needed



Step 8

Serve the ginger/lime baked fish with your pineapple rice Squeeze the remaining lime over the fish Enjoy!
