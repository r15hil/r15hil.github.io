Bacon Wrapped Chicken With Lentils

NOTICE: All of these recipes have been retrieved from the Gousto API. I do not claim ownership of any recipes in these files. These recipes are provided for informational purposes only, based on publicly available data from Gousto.

Description: Wrapping chicken in bacon not only keeps the meat moist – it also acts as a seasoning. We love these pre-cooked lentils: they’re packed with protein and are simply stirred through with a splash of Shaoxing wine and a touch of cream at the last minute. Chicken-y, vegetable-y, lentil-y goodness.

Ingredients

  • 20g parsley
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 tin of lentils
  • 135g British dry cured streaky bacon
  • 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 40g Cornish clotted cream
  • 1 British chicken breast fillet
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 celery stick
  • 200g carrots
  • 1 red onion


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the grill to a high heat  Cut the chicken breast[s] in half down the centre





Step 2

Lay the bacon onto a lightly oiled baking tray (use tin foil to avoid mess!) in rashers of 3  Lay each piece of chicken over the bacon and wrap it up (see picture) Put the tray directly under the grill and cook for 10-15 min or until the bacon has cooked, crisped and the chicken is cooked through (the juices run clear when cooked)





Step 3

Meanwhile, dice the celery Peel and dice the carrots Peel and dice the red onion[s]





Step 4

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] vegetable oil over a medium-high heat Once hot, add the diced celery, carrot, onion and bay leaf[ves] with a large pinch of salt Cook for 5-10 min or until softened





Step 5

Meanwhile, chop the parsley roughly, including the stalks  Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic





Step 6

Once the vegetables have softened, add the chopped garlic and cook for 3 min further Add the Shaoxing wine and cook for 1 min further Add the lentils (no need to drain) and cook for a further 2 min or until warmed through





Step 7

Transfer the cooked chicken to a clean board and leave it to cool slightly Remove the lentils and vegetables from the heat and stir in the clotted cream until it's melted  Stir in the chopped parsley and season generously with salt and pepper 



Step 8

Remove the bay leaf[ves] from the vegetables  Slice the chicken with a sharp knife and serve it over the lentils Enjoy!
