Drain and rinse your black beans





Step 5

Add the drained black beans, your tomato paste, ground cumin and ground smoked paprika to the pan and cook for 30 secs Add your soy sauce, 100ml [150ml] [200ml] boiled water and a generous pinch of salt and cook for 3 min or until thickened – these are your smoky beans





Step 6

While the smoky beans are cooking, add your tortillas to a plate Put the plate in the microwave and cook for 20 secs on high or until warmed through





Step 7

Add a squeeze of your lime[s] into the smoky beans Chop the remaining lime into 1 wedge per person Wash your lettuce, then pat it dry with kitchen paper and shred



Step 8

Divide the smoky beans between the warmed tortillas and top with the shredded lettuce and your smashed avocado Drizzle over the chipotle cream Garnish with a lime wedge Enjoy!
