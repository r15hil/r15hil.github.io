Creamy Vegetable Gnocchi

Description: Vibrant green broccoli and crunchy red pepper form the basis for this seasoned gnocchi dish, finished with tangy crème fraîche and a touch of spring onion. Delish!

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 400g gnocchi
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 200g organic creme fraiche
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 broccoli
  • 1 red pepper


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle Deseed the pepper[s] (scrape the seeds out with a teaspoon) and cut into thin strips Cut the broccoli into small florets





Step 2

Heat a large, wide-based pan with a matching lid (preferably non-stick) over a high heat with 2 tbsp [4 tbsp] vegetable oil Once hot, add the peppers and broccoli with a pinch of sugar, salt and pepper Cover and cook for approx 4-6 min





Step 3

Meanwhile, dissolve the stock cube[s] in 3 tbsp [6 tbsp] of hot water to make a concentrated stock Add the concentrated stock to the pan with the vegetables with a knob of butter  Reduce the heat to medium-low and cook, uncovered, for a further 3-4 min





Step 4

Meanwhile, add the gnocchi to a pot of salted boiling water over a high heat Cook for 3-4 min  Once cooked, drain the gnocchi and return it to the pot 





Step 5

Meanwhile, chop the spring onion finely, discarding the root end[s] Stir the spring onion into the crème fraîche 





Step 6

Add the drained gnocchi to the pan





Step 7

Add the crème fraîche and stir through Tip: for a lighter meal, just add less crème fraîche



Step 8

Serve the creamy vegetable gnocchi with a good grind of pepper Enjoy!
