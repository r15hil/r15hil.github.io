Ingredients

  • 1 courgette
  • 1 roasted garlic paste sachet (15g)
  • 35g grated Italian hard cheese
  • 1 bag flaked almonds
  • 160g quick cook spaghetti
  • 1/2 Knorr vegetable stock cube
  • 80g baby leaf spinach
  • 120g Tenderstem broccoli
  • 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 lemon

Step 3

Once hot, add the chopped courgette and cook for 4-5 min or until starting to soften





Step 4

While the courgette softens, add the quick cook spaghetti and Tenderstem broccoli to a pot of boiled water with a large pinch of salt and bring to the boil over a high heat Cook for 3-4 min or until cooked with a slight bite, then drain





Step 5

While the spaghetti and broccoli are cooking, dissolve 1/2 [1] Knorr vegetable stock cube in 200ml [350ml] boiled water Add the roasted garlic paste – this is your stock





Step 6

Step 8

Set the chilli & garlic Tenderstem spaghetti in the centre of the table, ready to share Garnish with the flaked almonds and lemon wedges Enjoy!
