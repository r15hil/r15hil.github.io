French Onion-Style Mac 'N' Cheese

Description: Add some French finesse to a comfort food classic. You’ll infuse mac ‘n’ cheese with clotted cream, before stirring through sweet caramelised onions. Top with a panko crumb, more cheese and bake till bubbling. Delicious.
