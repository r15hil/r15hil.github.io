Instructions



Step 1

Add your white long grain rice to a pot with a lid with 225ml [300ml] [450ml] cold water and bring to the boil over a high heat Once boiling, reduce the heat to very low and cook, covered, for 12-15 min or until all the water has absorbed and the rice is cooked Once done, remove from the heat and set aside (lid on) to steam until serving





Step 2

Peel and finely slice (don't chop!) your garlic Deseed your pepper[s] (scrape the seeds and pith out with a teaspoon) and cut into large bite-sized pieces





Step 3

Combine your cornflour with 100ml [150ml] [200ml] cold water in a small bowl





Step 4

Heat a large, wide-based pan or wok (preferably non-stick) with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a high heat Once hot, add the sliced garlic, chopped pepper and mangetout and cook for 2-3 min or until starting to soften





Step 5

Meanwhile, drain your king prawns Once the veg is starting to soften, add the drained king prawns to the pan and cook for 1-2 min further





Step 6

Add your chilli relish, soy sauce and cornflour mix to the pan and cook for 3-4 min or until the sauce has thickened and the prawns are cooked through – this is your sweet chilli king prawn stir-fry





Step 7

Once the rice is cooked, stir your black sesame seeds through – this is your sesame rice



Step 8

Serve the sweet chilli king prawn stir-fry with the sesame rice to the side Enjoy!
