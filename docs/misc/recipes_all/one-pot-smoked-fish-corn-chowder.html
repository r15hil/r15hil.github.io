One-Pot Smoked Fish & Corn Chowder

Description: Chowder is a hearty bowl of creamy fish stew - here smoked basa is paired with crunchy sweetcorn in this quick and easy, warming one-pot.

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 tin of sweetcorn
  • 300g potatoes
  • 2 smoked basa fillets
  • 10g fresh parsley
  • 1 spring onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion


Instructions



Step 1

Peel and finely chop the onion[s] Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic





Step 2

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a matching lid, over a medium heat Once hot, add a large knob of butter and the chopped onion with a generous pinch of salt and cook for 3-5 min or until the onion is softened  Add the chopped garlic and cook for a further 1 min     





Step 3

Meanwhile, peel the potatoes and cut them into bite-size pieces 





Step 4

Add the potatoes, the bay leaf[ves] and 500ml [1L] milk to the pan  Cook for 10 min over a medium heat 





Step 5

Add the smoked basa fillets to the pan and cover with a lid Cook for 10-15 min or until the potatoes are tender and the basa can be flaked   





Step 6

Meanwhile, chop the parsley finely, including the stalks Trim and slice the spring onion[s] finely Drain and rinse the sweetcorn





Step 7

Remove the bay leaf[ves] and discard Once the basa is cooked, flake it into bite-size pieces  Add the drained sweetcorn and half of the chopped parsley (save the rest for garnish) to the pan and cook for 1-2 min or until warmed through Season generously with salt and pepper -this is your smoked fish chowder 



Step 8

Serve the smoked fish chowder in large bowls and garnish with the chopped spring onion and remaining chopped parsley Enjoy!
