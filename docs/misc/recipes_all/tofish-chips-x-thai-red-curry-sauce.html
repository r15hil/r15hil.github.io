Step 2

Step 4

When the chips are almost done, drain your tofu, and cut the block[s] into three then each third in half again on a diagonal to create 3 pieces per person Pat the tofu pieces dry with kitchen paper, sprinkle with flour, then tap off any excess Dip the floured tofu into the batter and turn it several times until fully coated





Step 5

Step 6

Add your blanched peas, remaining chopped creamed coconut and a splash of cold water to a separate pot over a medium heat Once warm, mash the peas roughly until you have a mix of mushed and whole peas





Step 7

Step 8

Return the Thai red curry sauce to a low heat to reheat gently Serve the tofish with the chunky chips, coconut & lime mushy peas and Thai red curry sauce to the side Garnish with a lime wedge Enjoy!
