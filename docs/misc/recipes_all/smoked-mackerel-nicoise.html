Smoked Mackerel Niçoise

Description: Salade niçoise (French pronunciation: ​[ni' swaz]) is traditionally presented as a composed salad of tomatoes, tuna, hard-boiled eggs, olives, and anchovies, dressed with a vinaigrette. The Gousto version uses our locally sourced British mackerel and a punchy vinaigrette for a hearty and healthy dinner salad. Nice-oise!

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 60g black olives
  • 1 gem lettuce
  • 80g fine green beans
  • 1 tomato
  • 5.5g Dijon mustard
  • 100g British smoked mackerel
  • 15ml red wine vinegar
  • 1 British free range egg
  • 1 300g potatoes
  • 1 garlic clove


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle Cut the potatoes (skins on) into large, bite-size pieces  Trim the green beans





Step 2

Add the potatoes to a pot of salted boiling water and cook for 10 min or until almost fork-tender Meanwhile, add the egg[s] to another pot of salted water, bring to the boil and cook for 8 min Once done, cool the egg[s] by running them under cold water Tip: cooling the eggs completely makes them easier to peel!





Step 3

Meanwhile, chop the olives roughly Cut tomato[es] into wedges, then cut them in half  Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic





Step 4

Combine the garlic, red wine vinegar and Dijon mustard with 3 tbsp [6 tbsp] olive oil  and a pinch of salt and pepper - this is your dressing 





Step 5

Add the green beans to the potatoes, cover and remove from the heat Leave the pot off the heat for 4 min or until the potatoes are cooked and the beans are bright green and al dente (retaining a bite) Drain and return the potatoes and beans back to the pot, off the heat





Step 6

Add a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt and pepper to the potatoes and beans and give everything a gentle mix Peel and quarter the cooled egg[s]





Step 7

Chop the little gem lettuce into bite-size pieces  



Step 8

Assemble your salads with a layer of lettuce, the potatoes, green beans, then scatter the olives over, followed by tomatoes and egg Break the mackerel over the top (peel off the skin and discard), then drizzle with the dressing  Enjoy!
