Sizzling Chicken Cashew Stir-Fry

Description: This is a sizzling, sweet, hot, sensation! You'll use a Chinese cooking technique known as 'velveting'. It involves cooking meat in egg & cornflour for a moist, silky finish. It sounds challenging, but it’s surprisingly simple & makes this chicken stir-fry eggstra special!

Ingredients

  • 1 red chilli
  • 250g British diced chicken breast
  • 1/2 chicken stock cube
  • 130g basmati rice
  • 1 British free range egg
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 bag of cashew nuts
  • 3 spring onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 soy sauce sachets (16ml)
  • 1 red pepper


Instructions



Step 1

Add the rice and 350ml [700ml] water to a pot with a lid and bring to the boil over a high heat, once boiling, reduce the heat to very low and cook, covered, for 10-15 min or until all the water has absorbed and the rice is cooked Once cooked, remove from the heat and keep covered until serving





Step 2

Deseed the pepper[s] (scrape the seeds and pith out with a teaspoon) and cut them into bite-size pieces  Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic Cut the chilli[es] in half lengthways, de-seed (scrape the seeds out with a teaspoon) and chop ﬁnely Top, tail and slice the spring onions finely  





Step 3

Boil a kettle Add the chicken to a mixing bowl Dissolve half the [whole] stock cube in 50ml [100ml] boiled water





Step 4

Crack the egg[s] into the chicken Add the cornflour and a pinch of salt and pepper Mix well





Step 5

Heat a large pan (or wok) over a medium-high heat Once hot, add the cashews and cook for 3 min or until they are starting to darken in colour Once toasted, transfer them to a plate and reserve the pan for the next step





Step 6

Return the pan to a high heat with 1-2 tbsp vegetable oil Once hot, add the chicken and egg mixture Allow the egg to settle for 1 min then turn the chicken and cook for 2 min or until cooked through  Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside





Step 7

Return the pan to a high heat with 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] vegetable oil and the garlic Once hot, add the pepper and chilli (can't handle the heat? Go easy!) and cook for 1-2 min, stirring constantly Add the stock, reduce the heat to medium and cook until the stock has reduced by half



Step 8

Return the cooked chicken to the pan, add the spring onion and soy sauce, cook for 1-2 min then add the cashews Serve the chicken stir-fry over the rice  Enjoy!
