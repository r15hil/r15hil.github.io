Tuna & Olive Pizza With Herby Garlic Dip

Description: For this easy, cheesy, quick-prep pizza recipe, you'll top fluffy naan with creamy mozzarella, tuna, tomato and sliced black olives to create a tasty Italian-inspired family favourite. You'll also make a homemade garlic and herb sauce for dipping, serving it with a fresh tomato and basil salad to the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 garlic paste sachet (15g)
  • 3 mayonnaise sachets (45ml)
  • 2 plain naans
  • 1 can of tuna (160g)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 125g baby plum tomatoes
  • 30g pitted black olives
  • 10g basil
  • 125g mozzarella


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 220ºC/ 200ºC (fan)/ 425ºF/ Gas 7 Put a large baking tray in the oven to heat up (this will stop the pizza bottoms from going soggy!) Combine half the garlic paste with the tomato paste, a drizzle of olive oil, 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] water and a generous pinch of salt, pepper and sugar – this is your tomato base





Step 2

Drain the mozzarella Tear the mozzarella into bite-sized pieces, pat it dry with kitchen paper, pressing down very firmly so that it's really dry Drain the tuna





Step 3

Slice the pitted black olives in half lengthways





Step 4

Remove the heated baking tray from the oven and add the naan breads (use tin foil to avoid mess!) Spread the tomato base over the naan breads with the back of a spoon Dot the torn mozzarella over, followed by the tuna and the sliced olives





Step 5

Put the tray in the oven for 12-15 min or until the cheese has melted and the pizza bases are crispy





Step 6

Meanwhile, chop the baby plum tomatoes in half Chop half the basil, including the stalks Combine the chopped basil and tomatoes in a bowl with a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt – this is your tomato & basil salad





Step 7

Combine the remaining garlic paste with the mayonnaise, dried oregano and 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] water – this is your garlic & herb dip 



Step 8

Serve the tuna & olive pizzas with the tomato & basil salad and garlic & herb dip to the side Garnish with the remaining basil leaves Season with a good grind of black pepper and drizzle with some olive oil Enjoy!
