Add your quick cook spaghetti to a pot of boiled water then bring to the boil over a high heat Cook the spaghetti and the halved Tenderstem broccoli for 2-3 min or until slightly softened Drain the spaghetti and Tenderstem, reserving a cup of the starchy pasta water Reserve the pot

Step 7

Step 8

Serve the prosciutto & Tenderstem broccoli red pesto spaghetti Sprinkle over your grated Italian hard cheese and chopped basil Enjoy!
