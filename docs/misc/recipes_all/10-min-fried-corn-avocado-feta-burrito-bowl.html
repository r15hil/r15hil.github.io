Ingredients

  • 125g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 little gem lettuce
  • 1 can of sweetcorn (340g)
  • 100g Greek Feta Cheese Cypressa
  • 1 avocado
  • 25g honey
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 soy sauce sachet (8ml)
