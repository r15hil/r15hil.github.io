Cheesy Gochujang Potatoes With Fried Egg And Tangy Slaw

Description: Gochujang is the star of this dish. You'll coat waxy potatoes in this rich Korean chilli paste, before melting a generous layer of cheese on top. Serve with a fried egg and cooling lime-dressed slaw. Yum!
