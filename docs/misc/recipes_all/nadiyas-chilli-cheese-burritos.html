Ingredients

  • 80g Irish mature cheddar
  • 75g fragrant basmati rice
  • 10g fresh coriander
  • 1 tin of kidney beans
  • 200g British beef mince
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 pot of organic sour cream (114g)
  • 2 tsp ground cumin seeds
  • 6 white tortilla wraps
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion


Instructions



Step 1

Preheat the oven to 180°C/ 160°C (fan)/ 400°F/ Gas 6 Peel and chop the onion[s] finely Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic 





Step 2

Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) over a medium-low heat with 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] vegetable oil Once hot, add the onions and the garlic and cook for 10 min or until softened Once done, transfer the onion and garlic to a bowl (reserve the pan for a later step!)





Step 3

Meanwhile, add the rice, 175ml [350ml] water and a pinch of salt to a pot with a lid over a high heat and bring to the boil Once boiling, reduce the heat to very low and cook, covered, for 10-15 min or until all the water has absorbed





Step 4

Meanwhile, return the pan to a medium heat Once hot, add the beef mince and cook for 5 min or until starting to brown, breaking it up as you go Meanwhile, drain and rinse the kidney beans





Step 5

Once browned, add the onions and garlic back to the pan with the beef Add the beans, tomatoes, cumin, smoked paprika and a generous pinch of salt and pepper Stir and cook for a further 10-15 min, until thickened





Step 6

Meanwhile, grate the cheese Chop the coriander roughly, including the stalks





Step 7

Add the tortillas to a baking tray Put the tray in the oven for 2 min or until the tortillas have warmed through  



Step 8

Serve all the components in the middle of the table so everyone can build their own Enjoy!
