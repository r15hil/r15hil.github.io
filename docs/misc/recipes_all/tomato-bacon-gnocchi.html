Tomato & Bacon Gnocchi

Instructions



Step 1

Before you begin... Get all your ingredients and equipment ready Skim through these steps, then wash your fruit and veg Note: Equipment and pan sizes vary. Make sure you cook food thoroughly (even if it takes you a little longer than 10 min)





Step 2

Now, let's get started! Boil a kettle Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a drizzle of olive oil over a high heat Once hot, add your bacon lardons and cook for 5-6 min or until golden and crisp





Step 3

Meanwhile, chop your cherry tomatoes in half Chop your basil roughly, including the stalks Wash your salad, then pat it dry with kitchen paper





Step 4

Add your gnocchi to a pot of boiled water and bring to the boil over a high heat Cook the gnocchi for 3 min or until it begins to rise to the top of the pot Once done, drain the gnocchi, reserving a cup of the starchy cooking water





Step 5

Once the bacon is crisp, add the halved cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook for 30 secs





Step 6

Add your roasted garlic paste, tomato paste and your chilli flakes (can't handle the heat? Go easy!) and cook for 30 secs further Add your Chinese rice wine with a generous pinch of sugar to the pan and cook for 1 min further





Step 7

Add the drained gnocchi to the pan with a small splash of starchy cooking water and the chopped basil Season with a grind of pepper, give everything a good mix up and cook for 1 min or until the sauce begins to stick to the gnocchi – this is your tomato & bacon gnocchi Tip: Add a splash more starchy cooking water if your sauce is looking a little dry!



Step 8

Drizzle some olive oil over the salad and season with a grind of pepper Serve the tomato & bacon gnocchi with the salad to the side Enjoy!
