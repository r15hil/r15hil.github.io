Spicy Pork Meatball & Mushroom Ramen

NOTICE: All of these recipes have been retrieved from the Gousto API. I do not claim ownership of any recipes in these files. These recipes are provided for informational purposes only, based on publicly available data from Gousto.

Description: At Gousto we love smoky chipotle paste, which is remarkably similar to Korean chilli paste (but better!). We're pairing it with our moreish rice noodles to create a spicy ramen to go with our ginger pork meatballs, soy mushrooms and crispy sugar snap peas. (Dairy-free: see our FAQs for details).

View on Gousto

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 250g British pork mince
  • 1 star anise
  • 10g toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp chipotle paste
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 80g sugar snap peas
  • 30g panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 spring onion
  • 15g fresh root ginger
  • 4 soy sauce sachets (32ml)
  • 160g chestnut mushrooms
  • 100g Thai rice noodles
  • 1 brown onion


Instructions



Step 1

Boil a kettle Soak the rice noodles in cold water and set them aside to soften  Peel and grate the brown onion[s] Peel (scrape the skin off with a teaspoon) and finely chop (or grate) the ginger  





Step 2

Add the pork mince, panko breadcrumbs, grated ginger, onion and a generous pinch of salt to a bowl  Knead thoroughly with clean hands for 2-3 min, then shape into 10 [20] meatballs Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a matching lid with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium heat Once hot, add the meatballs and cook 6-8 min, turning occasionally 





Step 3

Meanwhile, tear the chestnut mushrooms into bite-sized pieces (this saves time and creates an interesting texture) Trim, then slice the spring onion[s] finely Slice the sugar snap peas in half lengthways





Step 4

Add the drained noodles to a pot and cover with plenty of boiled water and cook over a high heat for 4-7 min or until tender Re-boil a kettle  





Step 5

Meanwhile, once the meatballs have browned, push them to one side of the pan and add the torn mushrooms to the other side and cook for 3 min or until crisp  Once the mushrooms are crisp and the meatballs are cooked through, add 2 [4] soy sauce sachets to the mushrooms and cook for 1 min further  





Step 6

Meanwhile, once the noodles are tender, add the sliced sugar snap peas to the pot and cook for 1 min further  Drain the noodles and sugar snap peas and reserve the pot for the next step 





Step 7

Return the pot (no need to rinse it!) to a high heat  Add the chipotle paste, chicken stock cube[s], star anise, toasted sesame oil, remaining soy sauce, 1 tsp [2 tsp] sugar and 600ml [1.1l] boiled water to the pot, bring to the boil and cook for 1-2 min – this is your ramen broth   



Step 8

Meanwhile, divide the noodles and sugar snaps peas into bowls Top the noodles and sugar snap peas with the fried mushrooms and meatballs Pour the hot ramen broth over the noodles and garnish with the sliced spring onion and toasted sesame seeds and enjoy!
