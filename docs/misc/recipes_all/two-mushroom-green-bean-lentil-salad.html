Step 6

Step 7

Once done, add the caramelised mushrooms to the bowl with the salad, chopped basil, half the grated cheese (save the rest for later!) and the lemon-mustard dressing Season with a generous pinch of salt and pepper and give everything a gentle mix up – this is your two-mushroom, green bean & lentil salad



Step 8

Serve the two-mushroom, green bean & lentil salad topped with the remaining grated cheese Season with a generous grind of black pepper, sprinkle over your pine nut & seed mix and garnish with a lemon wedge Enjoy!
