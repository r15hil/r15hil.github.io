Ingredients

  • 200g pak choi
  • 2 wholewheat noodle nests
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 40g black bean paste
  • 80g chestnut mushrooms
  • 120g baby corn
  • 25g cashew nuts
  • 8ml soy sauce
