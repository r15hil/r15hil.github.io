## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp korma curry powder
- 250g steamed pure basmati rice
- 50g solid coconut cream
- 171g raw peeled king prawns
- 5g coriander
- 2 mini coriander naans
- 1 bag of ground almonds
- 30g sultanas
