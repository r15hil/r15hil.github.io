## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp dried dill 
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 150g bulgur wheat
- 1 chicken stock cube
- 4 British chicken thigh fillets
- 30g walnuts
- 2 tbsp French Dijon mustard
- 2 spring onions
- 1 celery stick
- 100g seedless grapes
